Movie Roulette

Can't you decide what movie you're going to watch?

Movie Roulette is an app that will choose a movie for you. The senario is well known to us all. An evening with your family, girlfriend or friends, where you want to watch a movie but nobody knows which movie. Here comes Movie Roulette with the solution. You open the app and get to see a random movie that you can see with the family, girlfriend or friends.
